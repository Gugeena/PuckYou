# 🧊 P#CK YOU!

> **Welcome to the coldest, most chaotic ice rink you’ll ever step onto.**

Puck You is a fast-paced **arena shooter built in Unity 2D** where every kill pushes time forward… and time is your real enemy.

You drop in, pick a fighter, and get thrown into momentum-driven combat where control is never guaranteed. Every elimination advances the clock toward **midnight**. When it hits, the real challenge begins — a brutal boss fight against the **Frostborne Curse: the Wendigo**.

---

## ❄️ Core Gameplay Loop

Spawn → Choose Character → Fight Enemies → Kill → Advance Time → Reach Midnight → Boss Fight → Defeat Wendigo → Break the Curse

---

## ⚔️ Features

### 🥶 Arena Combat
- Fast-paced, physics-driven movement
- Ice mechanics that constantly disrupt control
- Momentum-based fights where chaos is normal

### 👾 Enemy Variety
- Multiple unique enemy types
- Escalating pressure as time advances
- Increasing arena difficulty as midnight approaches

### ⏱️ Time System
- Every kill advances the clock
- Entire match is a countdown to midnight
- Reaching midnight triggers the boss phase

### 👹 Boss Fight
- Face the **Wendigo**, the Frostborne Curse
- High-pressure final encounter
- Survival and execution-based fight

---

## 🧍 Characters & Abilities

### 🔥 Berserker
Close-range chaos fighter built for aggressive plays.

- **Spin Attack** – Damage everything around you in a fast rotation  
- **Dash** – Burst movement that can parry the puck and redirect it into enemies  
- **Berserk Mode** – Forward rush that melts enemies in a destructive loop  

---

### 💣 Tactician
Ranged control specialist with explosive pressure tools.

- **Charged Puck Shot** – Powerful ranged blast attack  
- **Bomb** – Area denial explosive placement  
- **Airstrike** – Call down devastation over zones  

---

### ⚡ GlaceRunner
Speed-based assassin focused on raw momentum.

- **Trail of Fire** – Leaves damaging trails while moving  
- **Leg Dash Tackle** – High-speed dive attack into enemies  
- **Speed Surge** – Extreme velocity state for run-through eliminations

Puck You is a single-player arena survival experience where pressure builds with every kill. The entire game is built around controlling chaos, mastering movement, and surviving long enough to face the final curse.
